Precision at every scale — from structural logic to surface detail, grounded in the studio's 70-year family lineage. Leo brings deep expertise in computational design, fabrication, and technology strategy, working across architecture, environments, and objects with a precision that connects conceptual ambition to material intelligence.

Before co-founding the London studio, he worked internationally at Zaha Hadid Architects, Coop Himmelb(l)au, and Plus Architecture. He is a registered architect in the UK (ARB/RIBA) and Germany (BDA), and holds an MArch from the Architectural Association and an MSc with Distinction from TU Vienna.

Outside the studio, Leo sails — a discipline that sharpens a hands-on understanding of structure, force, and material in the world as it actually is.
