Leo leads creative direction and technology and innovation strategy at blng studio, working across architecture, environments, and objects. His work combines conceptual ambition with technical and material intelligence — a precision that operates at every scale, from structural logic to surface detail, and is grounded in the studio's 70-year family lineage.

Before co-founding the London studio, he worked internationally at Zaha Hadid Architects, Coop Himmelb(l)au, and Plus Architecture, developing deep expertise in computational design and fabrication. He is a registered architect in the UK (ARB/RIBA) and Germany (BDA), and holds an MArch from the Architectural Association and an MSc with Distinction from TU Vienna.

Outside the studio, Leo sails — a discipline that sharpens a hands-on understanding of structure, force, and material in the world as it actually is.
