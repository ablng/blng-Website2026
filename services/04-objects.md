We design furniture, fittings, and bespoke objects — as standalone commissions and as elements within larger architectural or interior projects.

Object design at blng studio is driven by fabrication logic and material behaviour rather than styling. We work with manufacturers and makers to develop pieces that are technically resolved and formally precise — objects that hold up at close range, which is where most designed objects fail.

Commissions have included bespoke furniture for residential and hospitality projects, and limited-edition pieces developed in collaboration with craftspeople and small-batch manufacturers. We also explore home objects developed through 3D printing — applying computational design to domestic scale.
