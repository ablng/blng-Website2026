We lead architectural projects from initial brief through to completion — across new build, retrofit, and adaptive reuse. Our directors are registered architects in the UK and Germany, and we take on commissions across both jurisdictions and internationally.

We work across RIBA Stages 0–6, either as lead architect, as design architect within a larger delivery team, or as competition collaborators depending on what a project requires. Clients come to us when they want design rigour and computational capability without the overhead of a larger practice.

Our experience spans residential, cultural, commercial, and civic typologies, with particular strength in technically complex or formally ambitious briefs.
