We work with landowners, developers, and investors at the earliest stages of a project — before planning, before a scheme is fixed, and often before a team is assembled.

Our feasibility work combines spatial intelligence with computational analysis: massing studies, yield assessments, planning context reviews, and design-led explorations of what a site can carry. We are direct about what is achievable and why — which is more useful to most development clients than optimistic drawings.

Our feasibility work is grounded in the UK and German planning systems. We are experienced with pre-application engagement, planning policy review, and design and access statement input — giving development clients a realistic picture before significant resource is committed.
