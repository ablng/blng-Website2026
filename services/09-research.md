We maintain active connections with academic institutions and engage in research that runs alongside our practice. Our directors have taught at ETH Zurich, Tsinghua University, and the Bartlett, and we remain interested in the boundary between academic inquiry and built output.

We collaborate with universities, research groups, and cultural institutions on projects that require design intelligence combined with theoretical rigour — whether that takes the form of a built installation, a written contribution, a funded research project, or a teaching partnership.

Past research has explored robotic fabrication, fibre weaving, structural networks, and computational form-finding — bridging material research and digital methods with built and exhibited outcomes.
