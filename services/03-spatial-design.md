We design temporary and permanent installations, pavilions, and spatial interventions — for cultural programmes, public commissions, festivals, and branded environments.

This is work at the edge of architecture and art: fast, material, and often fabricated directly by the studio. Installations allow us to test ideas at full scale, develop new material systems, and work with a degree of freedom that larger commissions don't always permit. They also tend to produce work that is genuinely visible.

We have experience developing proposals for open and invited competitions, and can move from concept to fabrication-ready documentation efficiently.
