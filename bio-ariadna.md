Ariadna leads creative direction and design strategy at blng studio, working across architecture, interiors, and spatial design. Her work is driven by concept — the idea that anchors every material decision, spatial sequence, and detail. Aesthetics is never the starting point; it is the outcome.

Before co-founding the London studio, she worked internationally at Heatherwick Studio in London and Architecture at Large in New York, contributing to large-scale cultural and commercial projects at the intersection of architecture, art, and design. She holds an MArch from the Architectural Association and a BArch with Distinction from Pratt Institute, New York.

Beyond the studio, she draws from art, fashion, and performance — fields where, as in design, the work only holds when idea, form, and expression are in alignment.
